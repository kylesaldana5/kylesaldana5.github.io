About Me

As a inquisitive individual, I have always had a passion for learning and solving problems. As a youth, these preoccupations were first sparked by music, science, history, puzzles, and games of any sort. This soon evolved into a greater interest in technology as I grew older and tech became more accessible.

In the fall of 2017, my curiosity and creativity were provoked by the possibilities of software development. I soon began doing tutorials, researching and reading anything tech. I quickly discovered that I had a new set of eyes on approaching problems. This way of thinking was different, challenging, and satisfying. I wanted to push myself and learn as much as I could about this field. I applied and was accepted to Nashville Software School. During my time there, I have learned so much so fast, while finding a new passion for the whole process of building and creating awesome and scalable apps. I've learned to love working with a team to solve problems and be creative. As well as venture of on my own, trying new things pushing my learning to new levels.

In my free time you'll find me coding, reading code, reading anything I can find on tech, most likely while listening to my favorite Phish jam. I also enjoy binge watching Netflix shows, playing poker, daily fantasy sports and seeing live music (but I haven't had much time for those lately). While my journey into the field of software development hasn't been a long one, this road has been full of self-discovery and so much learning. I excitedly await the next opportunity to show up, grow, and flourish..
